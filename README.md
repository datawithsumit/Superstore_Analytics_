# Superstore_Analytics_
**File:** `Superstore_Analytics_Sumit.xlsx`

**Objective:** Analyze 9,994 sales transactions to identify revenue optimization opportunities

**Key Features:**
- 5 interconnected Pivot Tables with interactive slicers
- Multi-dimensional sales analysis (Product, Region, Time, Customer)
- INDEX+MATCH lookups for automated data relationships
- Professional data visualization with 4 dynamic charts
- KPI tracking ($2.3M revenue, 9,994 orders analyzed)

**Business Impact:**
- Identified Technology as top revenue category (36% of sales)
- Discovered West region generates highest revenue ($725K)
- Analyzed sales trends showing 49% growth from 2014-2017
- Interactive filtering enables real-time business decisions

**Technical Skills:**
- Pivot Tables & Calculated Fields
- INDEX+MATCH, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIF
- Data Cleaning & Validation
- Interactive Slicers & Cross-filtering
- Dashboard Design & KPI Cards
